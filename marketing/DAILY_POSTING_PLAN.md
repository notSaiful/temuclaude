## CRITICAL FINDINGS THAT CHANGE OUR STRATEGY

### 1. LINK SUPPRESSION (URGENT)
Posts with external links get 30-94% visibility reduction. We've been putting GitHub links in every tweet — this is killing our reach.

FIX: Stop putting links in tweet bodies. Post the content, then reply with the link. Or say "link in bio."

### 2. DORMANT-THEN-BURST = BAN RISK
70% of account bans came from the "dormant-then-burst" pattern. We posted 6 tweets rapidly today after days of silence. This is the #1 ban trigger.

FIX: Slow down. Maintain steady daily pace. Never burst.

### 3. LONG-FORM > THREADS
Since January 2026, single long-form posts generate 40-60% more impressions than threads. Each tweet in a thread is scored independently — if tweet #1 fails, tweets 2-7 never get seen.

FIX: Use long-form single posts instead of 7-tweet threads. Save threads only for deep-dives.

### 4. REPLIES ARE THE GROWTH ENGINE
A reply is worth 13.5x a like. A reply that earns a reply back (conversation) is weighted +75. The 70/30 rule: 70% time on strategic replies, 30% on original content.

FIX: We need to reply to 20-30 AI/tech accounts daily. This is where growth comes from, not original posts.

### 5. BOOKMARKS ARE GOLD
A bookmark is worth 10x a like in the algorithm. Create bookmarkable content (frameworks, data, step-by-step processes). Explicitly ask: "Bookmark this for later."

### 6. X PREMIUM ($8/month)
Premium accounts get 1,550+ impressions/post vs under 100 for free accounts. Free accounts with external links get near-zero engagement since March 2025.

FIX: Get X Premium ASAP. It's the single highest-ROI investment.

### 7. VIDEO GETS 10x ENGAGEMENT
Native video gets ~10x the engagement of text-only posts. 4 out of 5 user sessions include video.

FIX: Prioritize video content — screen recordings of Temuclaude in action.

## THE DAILY ROUTINE (60-90 minutes)

### Phase 1: First 30 Days (0 -> ~300 followers)

**WARM-UP (5 min):**
- Scroll feed, like 10-15 relevant AI/tech posts
- This signals "human behavior" to the algorithm

**STRATEGIC REPLIES (30 min) — THIS IS 70% OF GROWTH:**
- Reply to 10-15 posts from AI/tech accounts (2-10x our follower count)
- Reply within 15 minutes of their post going live (early replies get 3-5x more visibility)
- Add genuine value: share data, tell relevant stories, offer different perspectives
- 2-3 sentences max per reply
- Include a hook: "I tried this and..." or "Counterpoint:..."
- Never reply "Great post!" — these get ignored
- Ask follow-up questions to extend conversation (algorithm rewards back-and-forth)

**ORIGINAL POSTS (15 min):**
- Post 2-3 tweets per day (NOT 6 in a burst)
- Mix: value (real experiments), opinions, stories
- NO LINKS in tweet body — put links in replies
- Use long-form single posts instead of threads
- Include specific numbers (3.4x more engagement)
- Ask for bookmarks: "Bookmark this for later"

**ENGAGE WITH REPLIES (10 min):**
- Reply to every comment on our own posts within first 60 minutes
- The first 30-60 minutes after posting = everything
- Turn replies into conversations (each back-and-forth = +75 algorithm weight)

**PROFILE CHECK (5 min):**
- Review which replies drove the most profile visits
- Adjust strategy based on what worked

### Weekly:
- 1 proof post (screenshot, benchmark numbers, results)
- 1 long-form deep-dive post (NOT a thread)
- DM 5-10 people who engaged with our content
- Review analytics and adjust
