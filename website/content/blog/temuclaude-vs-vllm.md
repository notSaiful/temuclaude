# TemuClaude vs Fable 5: The Benchmark That Matters

TemuClaude is compared against Fable 5 as the canonical quality and cost baseline.

## Fable 5 Direct

Fable 5 direct is the simple path: send every prompt to one frontier model and pay direct token rates.

## TemuClaude

TemuClaude is the orchestrated path: route, fuse, verify, retry, and show live progress. The system is designed for builders who want strong answers, API access, billing controls, credits, logs, and visibility into how each answer was made.

## Cost

Fable 5 direct costs $10/$50 per million tokens. TemuClaude's blended target is around $0.94 per million tokens.

That is the comparison the site uses everywhere.
